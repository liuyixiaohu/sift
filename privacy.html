Sift Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

What Sift Does

Sift is a Chrome extension that helps you filter LinkedIn job listings and clean up your feed. It runs entirely in your browser on LinkedIn pages.

Data Collection

Sift does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No data sent to external servers
  • No cookies set by the extension
  • No user accounts or sign-in

Local Storage

Sift saves your preferences (skipped companies, skipped title keywords, toggle states) using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local). This data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Permissions

  • storage — to save your filter preferences locally
  • Host permission (linkedin.com) — to read and annotate job listings and feed posts on LinkedIn

Third-Party Services

Sift loads the EB Garamond font from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy applies to that request. No other third-party services are used.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
